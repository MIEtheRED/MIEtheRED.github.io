Beard Oil, Balm, and Wash

We offer a variety of scents ranging from euphoric floral scents to dark and opulent woodsy scents. We trial our new scents on our crew first, so that we can personally testify of their magnificence.

Beard Kits

The Apprentice - (1) Oil and (1) Balm
The Wizard - (1) Oil, (1) Balm, and (1) Wash
Warlock's Box - (1) Oil, (1) Balm, (1) Wash, and either a comb or brush
Warlock's Box+ - Everything that is included in the standard box, plus a beard trimming set

Our Scents

Earth - A thouroughly grounded scent that finds it's base in the deep cedar woods, with notes of bergamont and amber.

Wind - A delicate, airy scent branching from the heights of the tallest pine trees, with notes of orange and fresh mint.

Fire - A spicy blend of agarwood, bergamont, and oakmoss that is sure to distract even the most cunning of foes.

Water - A euphoic combination that reaches from the depths of the ocean floor with floral essences, to break of the waves with tones of vanilla and coconut.
