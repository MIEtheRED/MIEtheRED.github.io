What does beard oil do?

A beard oil will help to promote healthy beard growth by allowing your face stay properly moisturized. The oil will penetrate deep into the follicle and saturate the root of your beard hair.

What does beard balm do?

A beard balm is meant to moisturize your beard hair. Think of it like a leave in conditioner that keeps your face smelling and looking great!
