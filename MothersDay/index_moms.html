MOMS

ARE AWESOME

From the moment you found out you were having a child, until now, your unconditional love for me has been ever present. It is hard to express exactly how thankful that I am to have had such a wonderful mother, especially when we are so far apart. I want you to know that I am who I am because of the hard work that you put in to make sure I was on the right path. Through your gentle reminders along the way, mistakes that I made never seemed like something that I couldn't fix. It is your excellent parenting that taught me how to be a great husband and father. I love you more than words could ever tell.
